! Damage
Demo

Temporary value, calculated after performing a hit.

  • For Attack option / Enemy

    Damage = Fighter ATK - Target (DEF + SPL * AGL * 0.005)

  • For Skills use

    Damage = Fighter SPL * SC * Target Skill WK

Can be found on the midle battle message panel.
