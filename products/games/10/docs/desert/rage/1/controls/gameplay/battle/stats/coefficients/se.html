! Damage
Demo

Temporary value, calculated after performing a hit.

  • For Attack option

    Damage = Fighter ATK - Target DEF

  • For Skills use

    Damage = DP * SC - Target DEF

  • For Enemy

    Damage = Fighter (ATK + SPL) - Target DEF

You can determine it by HP fading out.
