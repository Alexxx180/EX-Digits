Desert Rage

Mechanics.

Main game process divided onto 2 parts.

Labyrinth map searchs

During map searchs you need to find ancient artifact - sacred colored stone.

Then stand before and interact with it.

Random battle encouters

When you are walking on the level map, you can be encountered by monsters.
This is how battle starts.

Your goal is to end the battle.

To achieve this try escape from battle or defeat all enemies on the screen

To act in the battle - use options.

Then you need to wait for your turn before acting again.
